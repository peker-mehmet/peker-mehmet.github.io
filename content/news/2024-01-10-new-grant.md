I am pleased to announce that our proposal **"Metacognition and Academic Resilience in First-Generation University Students"** has been funded by TÜBİTAK under the 1001 Scientific and Technological Research Projects scheme.

The project will run from **February 2024 to January 2027** and will involve collaboration with researchers at three universities. Recruitment for the first wave of data collection will begin in the 2024 spring semester.

More details will be posted as the project gets underway.
