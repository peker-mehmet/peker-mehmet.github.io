We are delighted to announce that our paper **"Working Memory Capacity and Academic Achievement in Bilingual Students"** has been awarded the **Best Paper Award** in the Cognitive Psychology track at the International Congress of Psychology 2024, held in Prague, Czech Republic.

## About the Research

The paper presents findings from our ongoing TÜBİTAK-funded project investigating how working memory capacity shapes academic outcomes in Turkish-English bilingual university students. Using a longitudinal design over two academic semesters, we tracked 240 participants and administered a battery of neuropsychological assessments alongside academic performance measures.
